About Our Project

The project is called sitting posture nad safe screen-distance detection. The goal of this project is to detect incorrect sitting postures and unsafe distance from the monitor. Users will get an output indicating whether the sitting posture is correct or not and whther the distance to the monitor is safe or not. A window will pop up to users with green color indicating that the posture is correct. Otherwise, another window with red color will show up.
